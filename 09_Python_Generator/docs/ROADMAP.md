# 1. ROADMAP OVERVIEW

This roadmap defines the long-term development plan for the
Executive Marketing & Sales Dashboard project.

The project is organized into major development phases rather than
individual sprints. This approach provides a clearer view of product
evolution while allowing sprint planning to remain flexible.

# 7. MAINTENANCE POLICY

This roadmap shall be reviewed after every completed sprint.

Updates are required only when:

- A development phase is completed.

- A major architectural decision is approved.

- A new long-term objective is introduced.

Minor sprint progress should not require restructuring this roadmap.
